Multiple Route Mapping

When asked for navigation, multiple routes are shown to the user, including their distances.

Objective: Provide users with multiple route options.
Description: Users can view several possible routes between two locations, allowing them to choose the one that best suits their needs based on factors like travel time, distance, and current traffic conditions.
Benefits: Empowers users with choices, enabling them to select the most convenient and efficient route.

Shortest Path View

When asked for navigation, the shortest path is displayed to the user.

Objective: Provide users with the shortest route option.
Description: Users can view the shortest possible route between two locations, prioritizing the least distance. This option helps users save time and resources, particularly for situations requiring the most direct path.
Benefits: Saves time, reduces travel distance, and is ideal for users seeking the most direct route.
