Travel history and analysis

The travel history is stored in an external file and that data can be accessed by user or programmer at any time Objective: Track and analyze users' travel history. Description: The app maintains a log of users' travel history, enabling them to review past routes for safety and efficiency. Users can analyze this data to identify travel patterns and make informed decisions about their future travel habits. Benefits: Helps users optimize their travel routines, ensuring safer and more efficient journeys.

Adding new locations

It gives an opportunity to user or programmer to add new locations with thier distances between their adjacent destinations Objective: Update the city map with new developments. Description: Users or city officials can add new locations to the city map, such as newly constructed buildings, roads, or public amenities. Benefits: Keeps the navigation system up-to-date, ensuring accuracy and reliability for users.
